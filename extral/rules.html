WHAT ARE THE RULES OF ROCK PAPER SCISSORS GAME?

rules of Rock paper scissors:

Game objective

Beat the AI by selecting a hand siginal that outsmarts its choice

Hand siginals

  1. Rock (closed fist)
  2. paper (open Hand)
  3. Scissors (v-shaped Fingers)

winning Combination

  • Rock beats scissors rock crushes scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper (scissors cut paper)
  • Paper beats Rock paper covers Rock

Gameplay:

  • Choose your hand siginal by clicking one of the three buttons.
  • The Ai will generate itsown random vatual hand siginal move
  • The winner is determined by the winning combination above.
  • if both you and the Ai choose the same hand signal, it's a tie
  • if you use autoplay and score too high the Ai will feel like you are cheating and reset the scores
  • Autoplay feature doubles your scores by 2

Game Modes

  • infinite play: play an infinite round with Ai
  • Autoplay: run autoplay to play with Ai until the Ai gets tied

score keeping:


keeps track of your wins, loses and ties and save them for you to countinue form your last stop.
